3700 SERIES PIM INK​

3700 SERIES PIM INK is two-component ink for insert molding and in-mold-molding.It dries at low temperature and forms flexible ink film with good heat resistance. PIM is environmentally friendly low-odor ink.

Properties

PIM INK dries at low temperature of 80～90℃.
Ink film is tough and flexible, which is suitable for molding.
Good heat resistance that is required for insert molding and in-mold molding.
Mirror effect is not spoiled when used as backing of FUNCOAT Meta-Mirror Ink

Applications Usage

Polycarbonate/PET film for insert molding and in-mold molding, Polycarbonate for name plates

HOW TO USE

Please make the below mixture:
Ink…100
Hardener JA-930…3～10 (standard ratio is 6 parts)
#150～#200 thinner…10～30
When even print cannot be obtained due to static electricity, please use PA thinner up to 5%.

Drying

Tack-free drying …80℃ x 5 minutes
Standard drying …80℃ x 60 minutes

Physical Properties

  1. Ink…PIM 3790 Black（Meta-Mirror ink HG Silver is printed on the half of the material with the backing of 3790 Black）
  2. Thinner…#170 standard drying
  3. Hardener…JA-930(6％)
  4. Substrate…Teijin Panlite sheet 0.5mm
  5. Mesh…250/inch
  6. Drying…80℃ x 60min.
Test Item Test Methods Results
Adhesion Cross-hatch, tape-peeling test 100/100 no peel-off
Pencil hardness JIS K 5400 pencil scratch B
Heat resistance 100℃ 72 hrs No problem

PROPERTIES of MOLDED MATTER

Molded resin … Toray ABS　Toyolac 920 Natural

Test Item Test Methods Results
Humidity resistance 60℃ 95％RH 168 hours No problem on the appearance No Peel-off
Warm water resistance mmersion in 40℃ water for 48 hours No problem on the appearance No Peel-off
Heat resistance 70℃ x 100時間 No problem on the appearance No Peel-off
Heat-cold cycle test 70℃ → -30℃ 2 hours each x 10 cycles No problem on the appearance No Peel-off

Half of the first layer is Meta-Mirror ink HG Silver ＋JA-950(3%) with 3790 as backing. Other half is printed only with 3790 with no mirror ink underneath.

Precautions

  1. These value above are not guaranteed ones, but test results.
  2. The contents shown in this information might be revised without notice.
  3. JA-930 becomes solid by the reaction with humidity. Please keep the lid tightly closed and store it in a cool and dark place. Once the can is opened, the skim may appear during the storage. The product can be used by removing the skim if it’s before expiry date.
  4. The shelf life of JA-930 is one year after manufacturing date if unopened and three months after opening the container.
  5. Please refer to MSDS before use.
