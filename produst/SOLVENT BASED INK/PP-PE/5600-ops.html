5600 SERIES OPS INK​

Properties

Fast drying. Excellent adhesion. Chemical resistance.Pre-treated polyolefine sheets & bottles. Coated metal.

INTRODUCTIONS

5600 SERIES OPS INK is evaporative drying type of ink for polyolefin such as PP.

APPLICATIONS

Treated PP sheet, untreated PP sheet, rigid polyolefin such as treated PE bottles, primed metal. (PP=polypropylene,PE=polyethylene)

CHARACTERISTICS

  • Good adhesion to polyolefin.
  • Ink film is tough and resistant to abrasion.
  • Excellent resistance to oil, alcohol and chemicals.
  • Good resistance to water and humidity.

HOW TO USE

Add THINNER(#170 standard drying, #200 slow drying or #250 retarder) and mix well before print. It is advised to do pre-treatment such as corona discharge or flame treatment for polyolefin. Adhesion is improved.

DRYING

THINNER is added by 10% and the mixture is printed on PP sheet.

  • #170 : Tack-free time, Approx.10 minutes (20°C)(68°F)
  • #200 : Tack-free time, Approx.15 minutes (20°C)(68°F)
  • #250 : Tack-free time, Approx.20 minutes (20°C)(68°F)

The ink dries completely by forced drying(60°C(140°F), within 5 minutes). It is advised to do forced drying as better adhesion can be obtained.

WASHING UP

Use #150 fast drying thinner (or VINYL CLEANING THINNER).

LIGHTFASTNESS

7-8 grades (Please refer to lightfastness chart)

COLORS & TYPE OF PACKING

Standard 16 colors. 1kg can.

PRECAUTIONS

  • 5600 SERIES does not adhere well to untreated PP. When the material is untreated PP, add JA-P16 to the ink by 5-10%. Although adhesion is improve by additng JA-P16, printability and gloss degrade.
  • 5600 SERIES OPS INK is not suitable to print on flexible polyolefin such as polyethylene film or foamed styrene.
  • When the sufficient adhesion or properties cannot be obtained by 5600 SERIES in the industrial applications, use 1000 SERIES EPILITE INK or 2000 SERIES AP INK or APG INK after pre-treating the substrate by flame or corona discharge.
  • Please refer to MSDS before use.
  • Be sure to do trial print before commercial run to confirm that the product fits the purposes.

PHYSICAL PROPERTIES

Substrate : PP molds
Ink : 5601 WHITE(#170 is added to 5601 before printing. Printed matter is left at room temperature for 48 hours.)
