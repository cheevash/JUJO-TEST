1700 SERIES HIT INK

INTRODUCTIONS

1700 SERIES HIT INK has superior heat resistance. HIT INK is suitable for automobile name plates, the panels of vending machines that are vacuum-molded and sign boards with electric lamps.

APPLICATIONS

Polycarbonate, acrylic cast plate, ABS, rigid vinyls.

CHARACTERISTICS

  • Excellent resistance to blocking and heat.
  • Strong ink film has good resistance to impact, bending and punching. Processability after print is good.
  • Superior weathering resistance.
  • Excellent resistance to oil, alcohol, and chemicals.
  • Lifting phenomenon doesn’t occur in case of multiple-layer printing.

HOW TO USE

Dilute the ink with PC THINNER. (STANDARD DRYING, SLOW DRYING, and FAST DRYING) Choose the most suitable type for the printing conditions. SPECIAL RETARDAR is recommended to make drying slower.

STENCIL & WASHING UP

Approx. 6-12 hours at room temperature (25°C / 77°F).

COLOR RANGE & TYPE OF PACKING

Standard 20 colors, Transparent 5 colors, EXO(extra opaque) 6 colors, matting agent. 1kg can

PRECAUTIONS

  • Tackfree time means as follows: Dilute HIT INK 1701 WHITE with 15% THINNER. Print with SOLID PRINT stencil. (6 x 6 cm, 7250 meshes/inch).
  • When the printed surface is touched with a finger, the trace of the finger does not show.
  • Tackfree time table above is not guaranteed. Actual tackfree time is changed depending on the printing conditions such as coating thickness and how many layers are printed.
  • Although ink dries at room temperature, heat drying is recommended to maximize ink properties.
  • When used on polycarbonate, ink penetrates into the material, which makes tackfree time short. On the contrary, it takes longer to get complete drying.
  • There are no problems for transparent polycarbonate and transparent acrylic cast plate although cracking problems might happen to injections, colored materials, and molded materials. It is advised that test printing should be done for these materials to determine the most suitable countermeasures such as annealing.
  • HIT INK gives a hard ink film. "8000 SERIES PC INK" is recommended for flexibility.
  • HIT INK has a comparatively strong odor. "8100 SERIES SNAP INK" is recommended for lower odor.
  • Please refer to MSDS before use.
  • Be sure to do trial print before commercial run to confirm that the product fits the purpose.
